## How to Use:

1. **Go to Admin Panel:** https://Sema3ny.vercel.app/admin
2. **Login** with your credentials
3. **Create a Study Level** (e.g., "Grade 1")
4. **Create a Unit** (e.g., "Basic Vocabulary")
5. **Create a Lesson** (e.g., "Common Nouns")
6. **Click "Add Words"** button
7. **Paste the JSON** from any section above
8. **Click "Add All Words"**
9. **Done!** Test the word cards
